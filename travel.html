Travel

I count myself lucky as I have been able to travel around the globe since a relatively early age, most recently I have been to Japan. While travelling I love delving into the culture and food of the country I am visiting. I would say that my best and most fulfilling travel experience was my World Challenge where we helped to renovate a school and build a farm. With that in mind, my favorite part of that adventure was a four day hiking and climbing to the top of a mountain in Namibia.
