Pets


At present I have a few pets under my care, my favorite, is leo-pard, the leopard gecko, he is extremely chill and loves to eat crickets. I also have a fish tank with fish and shrimp, as well as a hamster who is crazy about coconuts.

If it were up to my brothers and my girlfirend we would also have a dog, but I will only consider one when we are living closer to nature.

Leo-pard the
Leopard gecko


This is Leo-pard, he enjoys basking in the sun and eating crickets. He recently shed his skin and this is very beneficial and means he is growing well.

Reptiles need heat sources and are unable to generate their own heat, in the wild they would be relient on the sun.

Moley the
Syrian hamster


Moley is the second hamster that I have taken care of since coming back to Hong Kong, as I said before he loves coconut and running on his wheel during the early hours.
